Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

How to make a superb peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Continue reading below for instructions on how to make a simple peanut butter and jelly sandwich. First we'll start with the ingredients then move onto the required steps for assembly!

Things needed:

  • Bread of your choice (I suggest white bread)
  • Peanut butter
  • Strawberry jam
  • A knife
  • A plate

Assembly:

  1. First take out two slices of your bread.
  2. Remove the lid from your peanut butter and spread a good layer on both pieces of bread using your knife
  3. Next, spread your favorite jam on top of the peanut butter on one of the bread slices
  4. Put the slice with only peanut butter on top of the slice that has jam and peanut butter
  5. Optionally: Cut the sandwich in half (rectangle or triangle)
  6. EAT!
