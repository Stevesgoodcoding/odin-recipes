Lasagna

How to make a delicious lasagna at home

Below you will find the ingredients list needed in order to make this superb lasagna at home. There are many variations of lasagna out there, but today well be concentrating on making a nice simple dish.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

    Heres how to assemble it
  1. Place a lasagna noodle on the bottom of your dish
  2. Pour some tomato sauce on top of the noodle
  3. Put a large amount of cheese on top
  4. Continue making your lasagna by repeating the above steps

Now that you've made your lasagna with as many layers as you like, go ahead and place it in the oven at 350F for 30min.
