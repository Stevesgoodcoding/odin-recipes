Hotdogs

Making the perfect hotdog is easier than it seems!

There are many different ways to cook and serve a hotdog; over the fire, boiled, steamed, cut-up etc... Today we're going to keep it simple by boiling the hotdogs!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite brand of hotdogs
  • Hotdog buns
  • One pot
  • Water
  • Tongs
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Fill one pot with a few inches of water
  2. Boil the water in the pot
  3. Once the water is boiling put the hotdogs in the water
  4. Let the hotdogs boil until they start to crack
  5. While waiting for the hotdogs to cook you can toast the hotdog buns in the oven
  6. Take the hotdogs out of the water with tongs and carefully put them in the hotdog buns
  7. Put your condiments on top of the hotdog
  8. Serve the hotdogs
